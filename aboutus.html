About Us

Hi, my name is Obehi, and welcome to ObsssBakesCakes.

Welcome to my cake-filled world! I’m Obehi, the baker behind ObsssBakesCakes, where I’m excited to share my sweet journey of creating cakes, cupcakes, and more. From delicate hand-piped flowers to geeky tech-themed cakes, I’m constantly experimenting with new ideas and flavors in the kitchen.

Baking has been a rewarding adventure, and while I’ve made some delicious masterpieces—like my incredibly moist carrot cake and striking red velvet cake with a motherboard theme—it hasn’t always been smooth sailing. There have been plenty of hiccups along the way (Cricut sticker-making, anyone?). But, we’re all human here, and those baking fails have only made me better.

This is more than just a place for cake photos; it’s a real, behind-the-scenes look at my baking successes and my missteps. Join me as I explore new techniques, share my favorite recipes, and embrace the learning process, one cake at a time.
